Data Retention and Deletion Policy (Draft)

Objectives

- Retain data only as long as required.
- Support subject access and deletion requests.
- Ensure secure disposal.

Retention Guidelines

- Clinical records retained per jurisdictional requirements.
- Operational logs retained per audit policy.
- Backups retained with documented lifecycle policy.

Deletion and Anonymization

- Requests are tracked and approved.
- Deletion is verified and logged.
- Where required, data is anonymized instead of deleted.
