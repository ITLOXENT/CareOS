DCB0129 Clinical Safety Plan (Draft)

Scope
This plan outlines the approach to clinical safety management for CareOS.

Roles and Responsibilities

- Clinical Safety Officer: appointed, accountable for safety case.
- Product Lead: ensures safety requirements are prioritized.
- Engineering Lead: ensures technical mitigations are implemented.

Safety Management Activities

- Hazard identification and tracking.
- Safety requirements definition and verification.
- Safety risk assessment and acceptance.
- Safety case maintenance.

Hazard Log

- Location: pending (replace with specific repo path).
- Contents: hazard description, severity, likelihood, mitigations, residual risk.

Safety Requirements

- Traceability: link each requirement to a hazard and test evidence.

Safety Case Evidence

- Evidence artifacts: hazard log, risk assessments, test results, audit logs.
- Evidence generation: CI test reports, audit log exports, release checklists.

Review Cadence

- Clinical safety review before each release.
- Annual review of clinical safety plan and hazard log.
