Incident Response Runbook

Purpose
Provide actionable steps for handling incidents in CareOS.

Immediate response
1) Declare incident and assign an Incident Commander.
2) Establish a dedicated channel and start a timeline.
3) Identify impacted services and current blast radius.

Containment
1) Reduce exposure (feature flags, disable endpoints, or scale down).
2) Preserve logs and evidence.
3) Notify stakeholders with known impact.

Recovery
1) Roll back or deploy fix.
2) Validate service health and smoke tests.
3) Monitor for regression.

Post-incident
1) Capture root cause, contributing factors, and action items.
2) Update runbooks and test cases.
3) Close out with stakeholders.
