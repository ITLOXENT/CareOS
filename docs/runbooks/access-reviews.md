Access Reviews Runbook

Purpose
Ensure access is reviewed and least privilege is enforced.

Review cadence
- Monthly review for privileged accounts.
- Quarterly review for all staff access.

Steps
1) Export current access lists from IAM and application roles.
2) Identify unused or stale accounts.
3) Validate access against role requirements.
4) Revoke or downgrade access where necessary.
5) Record outcomes in audit logs.

Evidence
- Review report with approvers and timestamps.
- Revocation actions and updated access list.
